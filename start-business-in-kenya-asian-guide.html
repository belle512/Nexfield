How Asians Can Start a Business in Kenya – Step-by-Step Guide (2025)

From business registration to legal permits and cultural insights — Nexfield Africa shares the essentials for Asian entrepreneurs entering the Kenyan market.

Why Kenya is a Gateway for Asian Entrepreneurs

Kenya is one of Africa's most dynamic economies with growing demand for services, infrastructure, and innovation. Asian investors are exploring Kenya due to its position as a regional trade hub with access to East and Central Africa.

How to Register a Business in Kenya

  1. Reserve a company name via eCitizen
  2. Apply for company registration with the Business Registration Service (BRS)
  3. Register with Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA)
  4. Secure business permits and work visas (Class G Permit)

Popular Business Ideas for Asian Founders

  • Fulfillment & Warehousing
  • Wholesale Distribution
  • Asian Restaurants / Specialty Foods
  • Retail & Electronics
