Our Comprehensive Services

End-to-end logistics and fulfillment solutions for your African operations

Market Entry & Expansion Strategy

  • Feasibility analysis for logistics or e-commerce market entry in Kenya
  • Strategic roadmap for fulfillment expansion and last-mile delivery
  • Guidance on regulatory landscape for foreign logistics startups

Business Registration & Legal Compliance

  • Assist with Kenyan business registration (sole proprietorship, limited company, etc.)
  • Guide on obtaining logistics-related licenses (transport, KRA, NEMA, etc.)
  • Partner referral for legal and tax filing (KRA PIN, VAT, NHIF, NSSF)

Fulfillment & Logistics Setup

  • Design custom fulfillment systems (centralized, decentralized, dropshipping)
  • Set up partnerships with local courier services, 3PLs, and warehousing providers
  • Support in launching or expanding delivery infrastructure

Sourcing Support & Supplier Networks

  • Identify and connect clients with verified local or regional product suppliers
  • Assist in developing supply chain flow and vendor relationship strategies

Cross-Border Logistics Strategy

  • Provide customs and clearance guidance for regional deliveries (EAC-focused)
  • Advise on cross-border delivery setups from Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, etc.
  • Develop cost-effective import/export logistics solutions

Payment Gateway Integration Support

  • Recommend and assist with setting up local payment options (M-Pesa, card, bank)
  • Support international startups in adapting to Kenya's digital payment systems

Data Analytics & Optimization

  • Analyze delivery performance, client behavior, and fulfillment efficiency
  • Monthly or quarterly business intelligence reports (KPIs, failed deliveries, trends)
  • Recommend cost-reduction strategies based on data

Customer Feedback & Retention Strategy

  • Design and manage feedback collection systems (WhatsApp, Google Forms)
  • Build reports to help logistics firms improve service quality and retention
